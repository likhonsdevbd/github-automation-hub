## Research Strategy
1. Start with comprehensive web search for each area
2. Extract detailed information from official documentation and authoritative sources
3. Analyze tool capabilities, pricing, and integration options
4. Document findings with proper source attribution
5. Synthesize findings into actionable insights

## Success Criteria
- Minimum 15 high-quality sources from at least 4 different domains
- Detailed analysis of at least 5 tools per category
- Comprehensive coverage of all six research areas
- Clear recommendations and best practices
