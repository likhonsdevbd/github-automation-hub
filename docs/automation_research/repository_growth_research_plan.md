# Repository Growth Patterns Research Plan

## Objective
Research what makes repositories grow consistently in terms of stars, forks, and community engagement, focusing on six key areas.

## Research Areas

### 1. Content Strategies That Drive Growth
- [ ] Research successful repository types and content formats
- [ ] Analyze content quality factors that attract users
- [ ] Study README strategies and project presentation
- [ ] Investigate feature prioritization and value proposition
- [ ] Review case studies of rapidly growing repositories

### 2. Community Building Through Automation
- [ ] Explore automated onboarding processes
- [ ] Research bot frameworks for community management
- [ ] Analyze automated response systems
- [ ] Study community health monitoring tools
- [ ] Investigate automated contribution guidance

### 3. Documentation and Learning Resources
- [ ] Research documentation best practices
- [ ] Analyze tutorial and learning material strategies
- [ ] Study interactive documentation tools
- [ ] Review developer experience optimization
- [ ] Investigate multilingual documentation approaches

### 4. Open Source Contribution Patterns
- [ ] Analyze successful contribution workflows
- [ ] Research contributor retention strategies
- [ ] Study contribution guideline effectiveness
- [ ] Review governance models for growth
- [ ] Investigate mentorship and guidance systems

### 5. Social Media and Marketing Automation
- [ ] Research social media strategies for OSS projects
- [ ] Analyze community-driven marketing approaches
- [ ] Study conference and event strategies
- [ ] Review influencer and advocate programs
- [ ] Investigate automated content distribution

### 6. Analytics and Metrics Tracking
- [ ] Research key performance indicators for repositories
- [ ] Analyze tools and frameworks for metrics tracking
- [ ] Study growth measurement methodologies
- [ ] Review community health metrics
- [ ] Investigate predictive analytics for growth

## Research Strategy
1. Conduct targeted web searches for each area
2. Extract content from authoritative sources
3. Gather case studies and real examples
4. Analyze research papers and industry reports
5. Synthesize findings into actionable insights

Comprehensive analysis saved to docs/repository_growth_patterns.md
